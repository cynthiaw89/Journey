What is this?

The #100DaysOfCode challenge, asks developers to make a public commitment to code every day for 100 days in order to help build the habit of finding time to improve your skills. In addition, you should publish a journal entry daily for accountability and to motivate your peers. For more information, please visit #100DaysOfCode.
