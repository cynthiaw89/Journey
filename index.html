What is this?

I've always loved journaling. When I started my journey into coding, I found it helpful to journal at the end of the day to keep myself accountable for what I had accomplished, and what I needed to focus on next. I've also found many useful things that may help others on a similar path. Simply put, this is a page where anyone interested can easily follow my journey.
